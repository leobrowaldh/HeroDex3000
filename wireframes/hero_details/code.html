menu_book Biography

Bitten by a radioactive spider, Peter Parker gained the proportional strength, speed, and agility of an arachnid. With a genius-level intellect and his self-made web-shooters, he protects the streets of Queens and the world as Spider-Man. Guided by the principle that "with great power there must also come great responsibility," he balances the struggles of a regular young adult with the life of a global hero.
